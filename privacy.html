Privacy Policy

Torquple operates Ropter (ropter.io). App: app.ropter.io · Jurisdiction: India
Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Torquple (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates Ropter, available at ropter.io and through our application at app.ropter.io. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect information when you visit our website, request a demo, create an account, or use Ropter. By using our services, you acknowledge the practices described here. For contractual terms, see our Terms & Conditions.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, company name, and contact details you provide during registration, onboarding, or via the demo request form on ropter.io.
  • CRM data: Deal records, contact records, and activity data imported from your connected CRM (for example, Pipedrive) for the purpose of AI analysis, summarization, and delivering sales intelligence within Ropter.
  • Usage data: Pages visited on our website, features used in the product, device and browser information, and interaction data collected via cookies and analytics tools.

We collect only what is reasonably necessary to provide, secure, and improve the service, unless you voluntarily provide additional information (for example, in support conversations).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Ropter service, including AI-powered sales insights derived from your CRM data.
  • To authenticate users, manage accounts, and respond to support requests.
  • To communicate with you about your account, product updates, security notices, and marketing where permitted and in line with your preferences.
  • To analyze usage patterns, troubleshoot issues, and improve the platform and website experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights, users, and the security of our systems.

4. Data Storage

Your CRM data is imported, processed (including summarization through AI), and stored securely in our systems hosted on cloud infrastructure. We implement organizational and technical measures designed to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. We do not sell your personal information or your CRM data to third parties.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies on ropter.io to remember preferences, maintain security, and measure performance. We also use third-party analytics tools, including Google Analytics, to understand visitor behavior and improve the website. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; some features may not work fully if cookies are disabled.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. After account termination, we may retain certain records where required by law or legitimate business needs (for example, billing records). You may request deletion of your personal data or CRM-derived data by contacting hello@ropter.io; we will respond in line with applicable law and any contractual commitments.

7. Third-Party Services

Ropter integrates with third-party services including CRM providers (such as Pipedrive), cloud hosting, authentication, analytics (including Google), and AI or infrastructure providers as needed to operate the product. Those services may process data on our instructions and under appropriate agreements. Your use of third-party products is also governed by their respective terms and privacy policies; we encourage you to review them.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection and privacy law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, where it applies to our processing), you may have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal data, withdraw consent where processing is consent-based, and lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India or other competent authority as provided by law. To exercise these rights or ask questions, contact us at hello@ropter.io. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encryption at rest where appropriate for stored data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we work to continually assess and improve our safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy on ropter.io and revise the “Last updated” date. For material changes, we will provide notice as required by law, which may include email or a prominent notice on the website or in the product.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, contact Torquple at hello@ropter.io.
